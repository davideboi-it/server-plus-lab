# 1.1 Server Rooms and Data Centers

## Core Definitions
* **Server Room:** A small, on-site space used to house servers and networking gear for a single organization or company.
* **Data Center:** A larger, purpose-built facility with advanced infrastructure, including redundant power, advanced cooling systems, and strict physical security.

> **Note:** The choice between a Server Room and a Data Center depends on the organization's specific needs, expected workloads, compliance requirements, and long-term goals.

---

## Edge Data Centers
An **Edge Data Center** is a small, decentralized facility located geographically between central Data Centers and the end-users.

* **Primary Use Case:** Designed to provide ultra-low latency for time-sensitive services, such as:
  * Autonomous driving
  * Real-time analytics
  * Virtual Reality (VR)
* **Key Characteristics:**
  * Compact and largely autonomous.
  * Fully self-contained: Equipped with all necessary internal infrastructure, including racks, cooling systems, networking gear, and remote management tools.
